Refunds & Returns

Where's My Refund? — timeline tracker

A delivery-style status tracker that shows every state a refund or amended return has moved through — plus what's expected next — instead of just three vague stages.

Help & Support

Taxpayer Assistant (AI agent)

A live chat AI agent that uses tools — it looks up your account, fills out forms (8822, 2848), and faxes them to the right unit, narrating each tool call.

Business Tax Account

94x Online Signature PIN

Self-service wizard for a responsible official to request the 10-digit 94x PIN (Forms 940/941/943/944/945) and e-file straight to MeF.

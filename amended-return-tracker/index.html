Status timeline

Your amended return moves through three stages. We'll update this page as it progresses.

1
Received Apr 18, 2026
We received your Form 1040-X and added it to the processing queue. No action needed from you.
2
Adjusted Jun 27, 2026
We made the changes to your account that you requested. If the adjustment results in a refund, balance due, or no change, that information will appear here.
3
Completed Est. Aug 1, 2026
Processing is finished and a notice is mailed to your address of record. Any refund is issued about 3 weeks after completion.
What the stages mean. An amended return can take up to 16 weeks (sometimes longer) to process. Status updates can take up to 3 weeks to appear after you mail or e-file Form 1040-X. You don't need to call or file again while it's in progress.
